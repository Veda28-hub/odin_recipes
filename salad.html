HOW TO MAKE A SALAD

Ingredients

  • Lettuce leaves
  • Cucumber slices
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Combine lettuce, cucumber, tomatoes, and onion in a large bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well.
  5. Serve fresh and enjoy!
